Alternate Cursors

Customize what cursors you want to use. Below are the cursor-arrow, cursor-grab, and cursor-crosshair classes.

HUD

focus.js add HUDs for displaying additional information in the images such as the percentage or absolute locations.

Just set displayLoc to true to show absolute locations and displayZoom to show the current zoom level.

Zoom on Scroll

Enabling zooming on scroll is extremely simple. Just set zoomOnScroll to true.
